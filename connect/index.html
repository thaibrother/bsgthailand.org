CONNECT WITH THE PROVINCE

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel, Thailand Province, are part of a wider Montfortian and educational family. Below are our sister sites and the principal channels by which to reach the Province for inquiries, correspondence, and visits.

SISTER SITES

MONTFORTIAN.NET

Global Montfortian family

The global hub of the three congregations born of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort — preserving the writings, history, and worldwide presence of the Montfortian charism. English.

FSGTHAILAND.ORG

Saint Gabriel's Foundation of Thailand

The Foundation side of the Brothers' mission — six commissions, fifteen schools, and the governance of educational works across Thailand.

THAIBROTHERS.NET

Brothers' mission in the schools

News and reflections of the Brothers' apostolic life within the schools — how the Religious charism becomes visible in the daily work of education.

CONTACT THE PROVINCE

For correspondence with the Provincial Council or the Provincial Secretariat, please write to the Provincial House. Direct postal address, telephone, and email channels are being finalised by the Provincial Secretariat and will be published here.

For specific concerns:

  • Vocations — see Vocations · Contact the Formators;
  • Inter-congregational matters — addressed in the name of the Provincial Council via the Provincial Secretariat;
  • Press and media — please contact through the Provincial Secretariat with subject line clearly marked.
