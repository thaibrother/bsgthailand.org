WHO WE ARE

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel (Montfort Brothers of Saint Gabriel) form a religious congregation of pontifical right within the Catholic Church, founded in the spirit of Saint Louis-Marie Grignion de Montfort (1673–1716). Together with the Daughters of Wisdom and the Company of Mary, we share one Montfortian charism rooted in God Alone.

The Thailand Province has served the Church and Thai society since 1901, accompanying young people — especially the poor — in the path of virtue, learning, and faith.

OUR PATH IN THAILAND

A brief timeline of the Brothers' presence and mission in this land.

1901
First Brothers of Saint Gabriel arrive in Siam to teach at Assumption College, Bangkok.
1937
The Saint Gabriel's Foundation of Thailand established as a Thai legal entity to carry the Brothers' mission across schools.
Today
Approximately 72 Brothers serving in 19 communities. Mission continues across schools, formation houses, and pastoral works under the Foundation's care.

For the full mission history, see About · History. For the Foundation side of our work, see fsgthailand.org.
