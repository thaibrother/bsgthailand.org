CONTACT THE FORMATORS

If you sense the Lord may be calling you to consider life as a Brother, the first step is simply to write a brief message or to make a phone call. There is no obligation — only an invitation to begin a conversation.

WHAT TO EXPECT

A Brother responsible for vocation accompaniment will reply to you personally and arrange a time to meet — in person if possible, or by phone or video. The conversation is private, unhurried, and oriented to your honest discernment.

HOW TO REACH US

The contact channels of the Commission on Formation are being finalised and will be published here. In the meantime, you may reach the Province through the general channels listed under Connect.

Do not be afraid to write. The Lord who placed this question in your heart will accompany every step of the conversation that follows.
