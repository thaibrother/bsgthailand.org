HEAR THE CALL

"Have you felt the call of God to serve as a Brother?" If so, you stand at the beginning of a journey of discernment, prayer, and accompaniment — the same path walked by every Brother before you.

The vocation to be a Brother of Saint Gabriel is a gift from God — quietly given, freely received, slowly tested over years of formation. It begins with a question, and grows through accompaniment.
