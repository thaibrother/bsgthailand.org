PROVINCE ANNOUNCEMENTS

Official announcements from the Provincial Superior and the Provincial Council, organised chronologically. Each announcement is published in the voice of the Provincial Council in the name of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, Thailand Province.

RECENT ANNOUNCEMENTS

The chronological feed of Province announcements is being prepared by the Provincial Secretariat. Each entry will include: date of issue, subject, signatory, and link to the full text.

Categories planned for filtering will include:

  • Appointments and changes in pastoral assignment;
  • Annual Letter of Obedience (postings);
  • Convocations of Assemblies and Chapters;
  • Pastoral letters of the Provincial Superior;
  • Notifications concerning General Council communications.
These announcements are addressed first to the Brothers of the Province and to our friends in the Church — spoken in the name of the Provincial Council, with the simplicity proper to a Religious community.
