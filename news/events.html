MAJOR EVENTS

Through the year, the Province gathers for moments of common discernment, celebration, and renewal. These events shape the rhythm of our common life beyond the daily round of each community.

RECURRING MOMENTS OF THE PROVINCE

  • BSG Assembly — a periodic gathering of all Brothers of the Province for reflection, formation, and common discernment;
  • Provincial Chapter — convened on the schedule set by the Constitutions, to take stock of the Province and set its direction;
  • Jubilees of Religious Profession — celebrations of milestone anniversaries (25, 50, 60 years) of perpetual vows;
  • Provincial Retreat — the annual silent retreat shared by the Brothers;
  • Patronal feasts — Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort (28 April) and Saint Gabriel Possenti (27 February).

UPCOMING / RECENT

The chronological events feed is being prepared by the Provincial Secretariat. Each event will be published with date, location, and a brief summary, in the voice of the Provincial Council.

The major events of the Province are not items on a calendar; they are the visible threads by which a Religious family weaves its memory and renews its life.
