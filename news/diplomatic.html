INTER-CONGREGATIONAL COMMUNICATIONS

Letters exchanged between the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, Thailand Province, and our sister Religious congregations — a quiet but important strand of communion in the Church.

"That they may all be one." — John 17:21

WHAT THIS ARCHIVE HOLDS

The archive records the principal communications exchanged between the Provincial Council and other Religious congregations in Thailand and worldwide. Typical content includes:

  • Letters of congratulation on the election of a new Provincial Superior or General Superior;
  • Letters of condolence on the passing of a Brother, Sister, or Founder of a sister congregation;
  • Letters of solidarity in times of trial, persecution, or natural disaster;
  • Joint statements on matters of common concern in the Church and society of Thailand.

VOICE

All such communications are issued in the voice of the Provincial Council, in the name of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, Thailand Province — with reverence for the sister congregation addressed, and with the warmth of fraternal communion proper to Religious life.

The chronological archive of past communications is being prepared by the Provincial Secretariat.
