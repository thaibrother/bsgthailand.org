CHARISM · THE MONTFORTIAN SPIRIT

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel are one of three congregations founded in the spirit of Saint Louis-Marie Grignion de Montfort (1673–1716) — a priest, missionary, and Marian writer of the French Catholic tradition. Our charism is grounded in three inseparable convictions.

God Alone — Dieu Seul — nothing more, nothing less. The first and final word of the Montfortian life.

FOUNDATIONS OF THE CHARISM

1. GOD ALONE

Dieu Seul · the heart of all

Everything begins and ends in God. The Brother's life is consecrated radically to God through the vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.

2. THROUGH MARY

True Devotion to the Blessed Mother

Following Montfort's teaching, the Brother goes to Christ through Mary — the surest, easiest, and most perfect way of union with God.

3. FOR THE YOUNG & THE POOR

Educators in the spirit of the Gospel

Saint Gabriel's particular mission is the Christian education of youth, with preference for those who are poor or without other options.
