PROVINCIAL LEADERSHIP

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel, Thailand Province, are led by the Provincial Superior together with the Provincial Council — in communion with the General Council of the Congregation in Rome.

PROVINCIAL SUPERIOR

Major Superior of the Province

The Provincial Superior of the Brothers in Thailand — appointed by the General Council, serving the Province in spiritual leadership and governance.

PROVINCIAL COUNCIL

Five Councillors · 2026–2027

The Provincial Council assists the Provincial Superior in the governance of the Province through counsel and, on certain matters, consent (per Canon 627).

PROVINCIAL BURSAR

Temporal goods of the Province

The Provincial Bursar oversees the temporal goods of the Religious congregation, distinct from the financial administration of the Foundation.

GENERAL COUNCIL · ROME

Worldwide leadership · sister site

The Thailand Province lives in communion with the General Council of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, with its General House in Rome.
