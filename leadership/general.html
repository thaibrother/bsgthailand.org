GENERAL COUNCIL · ROME

The Thailand Province lives and serves in communion with the General Council of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel, whose General House is in Rome. The General Council, led by the Superior General, governs the entire Congregation worldwide.

THE WORLDWIDE CONGREGATION

Founded in the spirit of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort, the Brothers of Saint Gabriel are present in many nations across Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, and Oceania. Each country or region is organised as a Province — Thailand being one such Province.

The supreme assembly of the Congregation is the General Chapter, convoked every six years. Between Chapters, the Superior General and the General Council exercise ordinary governance.

COMMUNION WITH ROME

The Provincial Superior of Thailand:

  • Is appointed by the Superior General with the consent of the General Council;
  • Submits an annual report to the General Council on the state of the Province;
  • Participates in periodic gatherings of all Provincial Superiors;
  • Welcomes canonical visitation by the General Council on a regular basis.
Through this living communion with Rome, the Brothers of the Thailand Province belong to a Congregation that is at once universal (Catholic, worldwide) and local (incarnated in this land).

THE GLOBAL MONTFORTIAN FAMILY

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel are one of three congregations born of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort — together with the Daughters of Wisdom and the Company of Mary. Visit montfortian.net for the global hub of the Montfortian family.
