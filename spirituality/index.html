OUR SPIRITUALITY

The Brothers of Saint Gabriel draw their spiritual life from the legacy of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort — a spirituality grounded in God Alone, lived through Mary, and expressed in the daily rhythm of the Religious community.

MARIAN DEVOTION

Through Mary · to Christ

The heart of Montfortian spirituality — True Devotion, the Secret of Mary, and the practice of consecration to Jesus through Mary.

LITURGICAL CALENDAR

Feasts of the Province

The principal feasts the Brothers keep together — Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort, Saint Gabriel Possenti, and the great seasons of the Church year.

DAILY PRAYER

Rhythm of the community

The pattern of daily prayer kept in each community — Mass, Liturgy of the Hours, Rosary, and personal meditation.

RETREATS & RECOLLECTIONS

Times set apart

The annual retreats and monthly recollections that punctuate the year — for the Brothers personally and for those they accompany.
Our spirituality is not an accomplishment but a slow yielding — to Christ, through Mary, for the sake of those entrusted to our mission. God Alone.
