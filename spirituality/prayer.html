DAILY PRAYER OF THE COMMUNITY

In every community of the Brothers, the day is shaped by a regular pattern of prayer — the foundation on which all apostolic work rests.

A TYPICAL DAY

TimePrayer
Early morningPersonal meditation (mental prayer)
MorningLauds (Morning Prayer of the Church) · Holy Mass
MiddayDaytime Prayer (a brief office at noon)
Late afternoon / early eveningThe Holy Rosary (often in common, before supper)
EveningVespers (Evening Prayer of the Church)
Before retiringCompline (Night Prayer) · spiritual reading

THE FOUNDATIONS

The four pillars of the Brother's daily prayer are:

  • The Eucharist — the source and summit of the Christian life;
  • The Liturgy of the Hours — the prayer of the Church, prayed in common;
  • The Holy Rosary — the Marian prayer dear to Saint Louis-Marie;
  • Personal meditation — silent listening before the Lord.
Prayer is not an addition to the day — it is the day. Everything else flows from this hidden centre, where the Brother stands in silence before the One who has called him.

For times set apart from the daily rhythm — retreats and recollections — see Retreats & Recollections.
