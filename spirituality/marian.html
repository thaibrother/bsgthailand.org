MARIAN DEVOTION

At the heart of Montfortian spirituality is a profound devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary — not as an end in herself, but as the surest path to Christ. This is the great theme of Saint Louis-Marie de Montfort's writings.

Ad Jesum per Mariam — "To Jesus through Mary." The whole life of the Brother is shaped by this conviction: that the easiest, surest, and most perfect way of union with Christ is the way Mary herself trod.

CONSECRATION TO JESUS THROUGH MARY

Saint Louis-Marie proposes a way of life summarised in the act of consecration to Jesus Christ through the hands of Mary — a free, total, and irrevocable gift of oneself to Christ by way of his Mother. Many Brothers renew this consecration each year on a Marian feast.

For the writings of Saint Louis-Marie preserved by the Montfortian family worldwide, please visit montfortian.net/writings.
