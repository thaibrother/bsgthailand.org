IN MEMORIAM

In grateful memory of the Brothers of Saint Gabriel who have passed into the fullness of life. Requiescant in pace.

49 BROTHER MARTYRS · SPAIN 1936

Catalonia · Spanish Civil War

Forty-nine Brothers of Saint Gabriel and their chaplain, who gave their lives for the faith in Catalonia, Spain, during the Spanish Civil War of 1936. The Holy See has recognised their martyrdom — a step toward beatification.

BROTHERS OF THE THAILAND PROVINCE

The Brothers of the Thailand Province who have entered eternal rest are remembered in our community prayers and Masses. A detailed memorial list is being prepared and will appear here.

"Eternal rest grant unto them, O Lord, and let perpetual light shine upon them. May they rest in peace. Amen."
